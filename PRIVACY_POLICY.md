## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All your habit data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData, Apple's modern data persistence framework.

### iCloud Storage
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Only you have access to this data through your Apple ID
- We cannot access your iCloud data

### Subscriptions
- Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store
- We receive only subscription status (active/expired)
- Payment information is handled by Apple, not by us
- We never see your credit card or payment details
